- ซื้อประกันสุขภาพ AIA

Language:
Thai (friendly, trustworthy, semi-formal tone)

Goal:
Maximize conversion (lead form submissions, contact clicks)

---

2. TRUST SECTION

  - Experienced advisor

  - Real support (consult + claim help)

---

3. PROBLEM SECTION
- Explain pain points:
  - High hospital costs
  - Unexpected illness
  - Financial risk

---

4. SOLUTION SECTION
- Introduce AIA health insurance
- Explain flexibility, coverage, long-term value

---

5. BENEFITS SECTION (bullet points)
- High coverage
- No need to pay upfront (network hospitals)

6. LOCAL SEO SECTION

  “ตัวแทน AIA อุดรธานี”

8. FAQ SECTION (IMPORTANT for SEO)
Include questions:

10. FOOTER
- Short description
- Contact info
- Reinforce keywords naturally

Design style:

- Trust-focused (blue/green tone)
